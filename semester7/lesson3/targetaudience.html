Target Audience

Description

This website is intended to help people who want to know the weather, to choose their footwear accordingly.

Personas

Sierra

Sierra just got home from her mission and doesn't know what to wear for Idaho weather! She is used to it being hot everyday, without a lot of variation. She wants to know the weather so she can dress according to Idaho weather, instead of her mission weather!

Jordan

Jordan is a stay at home mom and has three kids that love to be outside. They love going to the park, zoo and anywhere that they can play on a playground. Jordan wants to know what the weather will be like so she can dress her kids up in the right clothes for the weather!

Scenarios

  • Someone just moved to Idaho and wants to know what kind of shoes she needs to go purchase for the weather here.
  • Someone is new to the area and wants to know where good parks are for kids.
  • Someone is visiting Idaho for vacation and wants to know what days they can go hiking.
  • Someone wants to visit for the weekend but wants to make sure they bring the right shoes for the weekend.
  • Someone is visiting and wants to know what shoes to wear for the weather to see what will be most comfortable for them.
