Room information

King

King rooms are perfect for couples, or a few friends traveling together. They provide one King bed, along with all of our specialized amenities and services.


Queen

Queen rooms are also perfect for couples, or a few friends traveling together. They provide one Queen bed, along with all of our specialized amenities and services.


Double King

Double King rooms are perfect for families, or larger groups of friends traveling together. They provide two King beds, along with all of our specialized amenities and services.


Double Queen

Double Queen rooms are perfect for families, or larger groups of friends traveling together. They provide two Queen beds, along with all of our specialized amenities and services.


Twin

Twin rooms are perfect for solo travelers, or a small group of friends traveling together. They provide one or two Twin beds, along with all of our specialized amenities and services.
